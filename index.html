About the Band

We Are "VENOMS GUITAR" — More Than Just Music

A group of artists who believe in the power of music to unite, inspire, and transform. From our first notes together to the thousands of fans we’ve connected with, our music has always been about more than just sound — it’s about creating a space for everyone to belong.We’ve spent years on the road, sharing our stories, our struggles, and our triumphs with fans all over the world. Each song we write is a chapter in our story, and we’re so grateful to have you along for the ride.
